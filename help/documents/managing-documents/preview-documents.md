## Preview a document

   <!--
   <li value="1" data-mc-conditions="QuicksilverOrClassic.Quicksilver">In a document list, hover over the row containing the document , then click <span class="bold">Document Details</span>.</li>
   -->

1. In a document list, hover over the row containing the document , then click `Document Details`.

   <!--
   <li value="2" data-mc-conditions="QuicksilverOrClassic.Quicksilver"> <p>On the page that appears, click the document's thumbnail image.</p>
   <ul>
   <li>If you have not prepared the document for review, it displays in a new browser tab.</li>
   <li>If you have prepared the document for review, the proofing viewer opens to display it.</li>
   </ul> </li>
   -->

1. On the page that appears, click the document's thumbnail image.

  * If you have not prepared the document for review, it displays in a new browser tab.
  * If you have prepared the document for review, the proofing viewer opens to display it.
