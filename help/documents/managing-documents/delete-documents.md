&#42;To find out what plan, license type, or access you have, contact your *`Workfront administrator`*.


To delete a document:



1. In a document list that contains the document , click on the document .
1. Click the `Delete` icon ![](assets/delete.png) above the Documents area.

1. In the box that appears, click `Yes, Delete it`&nbsp;to confirm.


A system or group administrator can restore a document within 30 days of being deleted, as described in [Restore deleted items](restore-deleted-items.md).
