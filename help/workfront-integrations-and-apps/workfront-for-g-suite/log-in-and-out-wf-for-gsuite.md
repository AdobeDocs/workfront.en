## Prerequisites

Before you can log in or out of `Workfront` for G Suite, you must

* Install `Workfront` for G suite  
  For instructions, see [Install Adobe Workfront for G Suite](../../workfront-integrations-and-apps/workfront-for-g-suite/install-workfront-for-gsuite.md).

## Log into `Adobe Workfront` for G Suite

<ol> 
 <li value="1"> <p>Click the&nbsp;<span>Workfront</span> icon <img src="assets/wf-lion-icon.png"> in the G Suite add-ons sidebar at the far-right of Gmail, Google Calendar, or Google Drive.</p> <p>If you have just installed the <span>Workfront for G Suite</span> add-on and you cannot see this icon, try refreshing the browser page.</p> </li> 
 <li value="2">Click <span class="bold">Log In </span> in the <span>Workfront for G Suite</span> add-on.</li> 
 <li value="3"> <p>Type your <span>Workfront</span> domain address, such as <i>companyname.my.workfront.com</i>.</p> </li> 
 <li value="4"> <p>Type your <span>Workfront</span> username and password, then click <span class="bold">Log in</span>.</p> <p>Or</p> <p>If your organization uses Security Assertion Markup Language (SAML) login, click <span class="bold">SAML Log In</span>.</p> </li> 
</ol>

## Log out of `Workfront for G Suite`

<ol> 
 <li value="1">If the <span>Workfront for G Suite</span> panel is not displayed, click the&nbsp;<span>Workfront</span> icon <img src="assets/wf-lion-icon.png"> in the G Suite add-ons sidebar at the far-right of the page. </li> 
 <li value="2"> <p>Click the <span class="bold">More actions</span> icon at the top of the <span>Workfront for G Suite</span> panel.</p> <p> <img src="assets/more-actions-icon.png"> <br> </p> </li> 
 <li value="3"> <p>Click <span class="bold">Logout</span> in the menu that appears.</p> <p>This does not log you out of&nbsp;G Suite.</p> </li> 
</ol>
