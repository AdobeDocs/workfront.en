## Install *Workfront* for G Suite

<ol> 
 <li value="1"> <p>Click the following link:</p> <p> https://gsuite.google.com/marketplace/app/workfront_for_g_suite/1076371296461</p> <p>Or</p> <p>Click the plus sign icon in the G Suite add-ons sidebar at the far-right of a G Suite app (Gmail, Google Calendar, or Google Drive). Search for <i>Workfront for G Suite</i>, then click the <em>Workfront for G Suite</em> app item that appears.</p> </li> 
 <li value="2">Click <span class="bold">Install</span>, then follow the prompts that appear.</li> 
 <li value="3">(Conditional) In your G&nbsp;Suite app, if you don't see the <em>Workfront</em> icon <img src="assets/wf-lion-icon.png"> in the G Suite add-ons sidebar at the far-right of the page, refresh the page.</li> 
</ol>

For information about logging in, see [Log in and out of Adobe Workfront for G Suite](../../workfront-integrations-and-apps/workfront-for-g-suite/log-in-and-out-wf-for-gsuite.md).
