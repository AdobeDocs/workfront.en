# View restored items {#view-restored-items}

As an *`Adobe Workfront administrator`* or a user with a *`Plan`* license, you can view which projects, tasks, issues, and documents have been restored in the past 7 days. 


For information about how to restore a project, task, issue, or document after it has been deleted, see [Restore deleted items](restore-deleted-items.md).

## View items that have been recently deleted {#view-items-that-have-been-recently-deleted}




1. Click the `Main Menu` icon ![](assets/main-menu-icon.png) in the upper-right corner of *`Adobe Workfront`*, then click `Setup` ![](assets/gear-icon-settings.png).  

1.  Expand `Recycle Bin`, then click `Recently Restored`.


   Items are sorted by the Restore Date by default.

1. Click the `Projects`, `Tasks`, `Issues`, or `Documents` tab, depending on the type of item that was restored.

1.  Click the linked name of the item&nbsp;that you want to view.


   The project, task, issue, or document is displayed.
