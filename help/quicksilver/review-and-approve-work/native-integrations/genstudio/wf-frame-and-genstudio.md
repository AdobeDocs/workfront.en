# Get started with the GenStudio for Performance Marketing and Frame.io integration

With the integration between GenStudio for Performance Marketing and Frame.io, you can 

* Use Workfront unified approval templates to define review and approval workflows

* Review and approve GenStudio for Performance Marketing draft content in the Frame.io viewer

* View review decisions in GenStudio for Performance Marketing for final approval and publishing

## Integration requirements 

* Workfront and GenStudio for Performance Marketing must be deployed to the same Identity Management system (IMS) organization.

* Users can belong to only one Workfront instance within the IMS organization.

* The Workfront instance must be enabled on the Adobe Unified Experience.

## Use Workfront unified approval templates to define approval workflows

If your organization's content review process is often repeated or is reviewed by the same people, you can use unified approval templates to automate review and approval workflows. 

### Create a unified approval template in Workfront

You can create simple, single-stage templates for just one or two reviewers, or you can create multi-stage templates for complex reviews with many stages and dependencies.

For more information about creating approval workflow templates in Workfront, see [Create an approval workflow template for documents](/help/quicksilver/review-and-approve-work/document-reviews-and-approvals/manage-document-approvals/create-approval-template.md).

### Choose or modify the template in GenStudio for Performance Marketing

When a user initiates a review in GenStudio for Performance Marketing, they simply choose the template they need. Users can easily change any unified approval approval workflow template, adding or removing reviewers and stages, at any time. 

For more information, see [Request review and approval](https://experienceleague.adobe.com/en/docs/genstudio-for-performance-marketing/user-guide/approve/request-review).

## Review and approve GenStudio for Performance Marketing draft content in the Frame.io viewer

You can review and approve draft content directly in GenStudio for Performance Marketing in the Frame.io viewer. 

With the Frame.io viewer, you can 

* Leave comments
* Mark-up draft to show what needs to change 
* Make a decision

For more information on using the Frame.io viewer, see [Commenting on your media](https://help.frame.io/en/articles/9105251-commenting-on-your-media).


## View review decisions in GenStudio for Performance Marketing for final approval and publishing

Once the asset has gone through the review and approval process, you can view the review decision and publish the content directly from GenStudio for Performance Marketing.

For more information, see [Publish approved content](https://experienceleague.adobe.com/en/docs/genstudio-for-performance-marketing/user-guide/approve/publish-content).
