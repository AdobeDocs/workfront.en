# Send a report in Reporting Canvas

You have the following options for sending an Adobe Workfront report:

* Send immediately
* Send once at a scheduled time
* Send on a recurring basis

>[!IMPORTANT]
>
>Add [specific email] to allowlist to avoid blocking report delivery emails that contain zipped files.
