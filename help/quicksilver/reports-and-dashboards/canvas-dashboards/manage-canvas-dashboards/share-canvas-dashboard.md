## Share a Canvas Dashboard

1. Click the **[!UICONTROL Main Menu]** icon ![Main Menu](/help/_includes/assets/main-menu-icon.png) in the upper-right corner of Adobe Workfront, or (if available), click the **[!UICONTROL Main Menu]** icon ![Main Menu](/help/_includes/assets/main-menu-icon-left-nav.png) in the upper-left corner, then click **Dashboards**.

1. In the left panel, click **Canvas Dashboards**, then click the name of the Canvas Dashboard that you would like to share.

1. Click the **Share** button in the upper-right corner of the page.

1. In the **Give access to:** field of the **Dashboard Sharing** box that appears, begin typing the name of the specific user, team, role, group, or company you want to share the Canvas Dashboard with, then select it when it appears in the list below. 

1. (Optional) After you have added an entity to the list of intended recipients, you can edit the kind of access they will receive for this Canvas Dashboard. By default they will receive only **View** access, but you can give them Manage access by clicking **View >** next to the entity, then selecting **Manage** in the dropdown list. Note that some entities cannot have their access level changed, such as administrators.

1. Once you have added everyone with whom you would like to share the Canvas Dashboard, click the **Share** button.
