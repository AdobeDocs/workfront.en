# Manage Adobe Workfront Planning in-app notifications

{{maestro-important-intro}}

When someone tags you in a record comment, you may receive an in-app notification for that tag. 

For information about tagging others in a record comment, see [Manage record comments](/help/quicksilver/maestro/records/manage-record-comments.md).

## Prerequisites

Your company must be a Adobe Unified Experience Workfront customer for you to receive an in-app notification when someone tags you in an record comment. 

To determine whether you are an Adobe Unified Experience user, see [Adobe Unified Experience for Workfront](/help/quicksilver/workfront-basics/navigate-workfront/workfront-navigation/adobe-unified-experience.md).

## Manage in-app notifications

1. (Conditional) After someone tags you in a comment on a record, go to in-app **Notifications** icon ![](assets/experience-cloud-notifications-icon.png) in Adobe Experience Cloud. 

    ![](assets/in-app-notification-example.png)

1. Click the notification. 

    The record details page opens in Workfront. You can make updates to the record, or reply to the comment. 

1. (Optional) Click **Mark all as read** to indicate that you have read all the notification.
1. (Optional) Click **View all** to go to the **Notifications** page in Adobe Experience Cloud.
