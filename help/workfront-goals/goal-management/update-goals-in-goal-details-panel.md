## Prerequisites

You must have the following before you can start:

* A Layout Template that includes the Goals area in the Main&nbsp;Menu.

## Update goals in the Goal&nbsp;Details panel

You can access an individual goal which opens the Goal Details panel of the goal from the following sections of *Workfront Goals*:

* *Goal List* 
* *Goal Alignment* 
* *Pulse* 
* *Check-in*

<ol> 
 <li value="1"> <p> <p>Click the <span class="bold">Main Menu icon </span> <img src="assets/main-menu-icon.png"> in the upper-right corner of your screen, then click <span class="bold">Goals</span>.</p> <p>This opens the <em>Workfront Goals</em> area. </p> </p> </li> 
 <li value="2"> <p>Click <em>Goal List</em>, <em>Goal Alignment</em>, <em>Pulse</em>, or <em>Check-in</em> in the left panel, then click the name of a goal. </p> <p>This opens the Goal&nbsp;Details panel on the right. </p> <p> <img src="assets/goal-details-summary-tab-350x294.png" style="width: 350;height: 294;"> </p> </li> 
 <li value="3">On the Summary tab, click <span>the <span class="bold">More icon</span><img src="assets/more-icon.png"></span>, then click&nbsp;any of the following options: 
  <ol> 
   <li value="1"><p><span class="bold">Edit</span>. For information about editing goals, see <a href="../../workfront-goals/goal-management/edit-goals.md" class="MCXref xref">Edit goals in Adobe Workfront Goals</a>.</p></li> 
   <li value="2"><p><span class="bold">Copy</span>. For information about copying goals, see <a href="../../workfront-goals/goal-management/copy-goals.md" class="MCXref xref">Copy goals in Adobe Workfront Goals</a>.</p></li> 
   <li value="3"><p><span class="bold">Activate</span>.&nbsp;This option is available only for drafted and inactive goals. </p><p>For information about activating goals, see <a href="../../workfront-goals/goal-management/access-goals-in-wf-goals.md" class="MCXref xref">Access and open goals in Adobe Workfront Goals </a>. </p></li> 
   <li value="4"><p><span class="bold">Close</span>, then click<span class="bold"> Close Goal</span>.&nbsp;This option is available only for active goals. </p><p>For information about closing goals, see <a href="../../workfront-goals/goal-management/close-and-reopen-goals.md" class="MCXref xref">Close and reopen goals in Adobe Workfront Goals</a>. </p></li> 
   <li value="5"><p><span class="bold">Deactivate</span>. This option is available only for active goals. This deactivates the goal immediately. </p><p>For information about deactivating goals, see <a href="../../workfront-goals/goal-management/delete-and-deactivate-goals.md" class="MCXref xref">Delete and deactivate goals in Adobe Workfront Goals</a>.</p></li> 
   <li value="6"><p><span class="bold">Delete</span>, then click <span class="bold">Yes, Delete</span>. </p><p>For information about deleting goals, see <a href="../../workfront-goals/goal-management/delete-and-deactivate-goals.md" class="MCXref xref">Delete and deactivate goals in Adobe Workfront Goals</a>.</p><note type="note">
     Deleted goals cannot be recovered.
    </note></li> 
   <li value="7"><p><span class="bold">Reopen</span>,&nbsp;then click&nbsp;<span class="bold">Reopen</span>. This option is available only for closed goals that are from a current time period. </p><p>For information about reopening goals, see <a href="../../workfront-goals/goal-management/close-and-reopen-goals.md" class="MCXref xref">Close and reopen goals in Adobe Workfront Goals</a>. </p></li> 
   <li value="8"><p> (Conditional) If you clicked any of the options between steps a-i above except Delete or Reopen, click <span class="bold">Save</span>. </p></li> 
  </ol></li> 
 <li value="4"> <p>Click&nbsp;<span class="bold">Align to another goal</span> in the upper-right of the Summary tab, then specify the name of a goal in the <span class="bold">Align to</span> <span class="bold">another goal</span> field that you want to align the current goal to. The current goal becomes the child of the goal you align it to. For information about child and parent goals, see <a href="../../workfront-goals/goal-alignment/align-goals-by-connecting-them.md" class="MCXref xref">Align goals by connecting them in Adobe Workfront Goals</a>. </p> </li> 
 <li value="5">Click&nbsp;<span class="bold">Add results</span>. Results drive the progress of your goal. For information about adding results, see <a href="../../workfront-goals/results-and-activities/add-results-to-goals.md" class="MCXref xref">Add results to goals in Adobe Workfront Goals</a>.</li> 
 <li value="6">Click <span class="bold">Add activities</span>. Activities drive the progress of your goal. For information about adding activities, see <a href="../../workfront-goals/results-and-activities/add-activities-to-goals.md" class="MCXref xref">Add activities to goals in&nbsp;Adobe Workfront Goals</a>. </li> 
 <li value="7"> <p>Click the <span class="bold">Updates</span> tab. Here, you can view goal comments and review the entire editing history of the goal, activities, and results, to understand who changed what and when. </p> <p> <img src="assets/goal-details-updates-tab-350x280.png" style="width: 350;height: 280;"> </p> </li> 
 <li value="8"> <p>(Optional) Deselect any of the following options if you want to not display them in the Updates tab.&nbsp;They are selected by default: </p> 
  <table cellspacing="0"> 
   <col> 
   <col> 
   <tbody> 
    <tr> 
     <td role="rowheader">Progress Updates</td> 
     <td>Displays information about the history of progress updates on results and activities. </td> 
    </tr> 
    <tr> 
     <td role="rowheader">Comments</td> 
     <td>Displays comments made by users on the goal. </td> 
    </tr> 
    <tr> 
     <td role="rowheader">Editing History</td> 
     <td>Displays information about creating and updating the goal, results, and activities. </td> 
    </tr> 
   </tbody> 
  </table> </li> 
 <li value="9"> <p>(Optional)&nbsp;Click <span class="bold">Details</span> under a progress or an editing history update to display additional information about the update. </p> <p> <img src="assets/update-details-in-updates-tab-expanded-highlighted-350x139.png" style="width: 350;height: 139;"> </p> </li> 
</ol>
