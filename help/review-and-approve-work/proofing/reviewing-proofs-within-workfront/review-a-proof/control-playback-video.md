## Adjust video playback speed

You can adjust the playback speed of the video *proof*. You can select to view the video between a quarter speed, to double speed.

1. Go to the project, task, or issue that contains the document, then select `Documents`.
1. Find the *proof* you need, then click `Open *proof*`.

<ol start="3"> 
 <li value="3"> <p>In the lower-right corner of the <em>proofing viewer</em>, click the <span class="bold">Settings</span> icon.</p> <p> <img src="assets/phq-viewer-video-playbackspeed--2-.png" alt="phq_viewer_video_playbackspeed__2_.png"> <br> </p> </li> 
 <li value="4">Click the current speed, then select a new playback speed.<br></li> 
 <li value="5">Click the <span class="bold">Play</span> button on the video to test the new speed.</li> 
</ol>

## View video frame-by-frame

For a more detailed look at the video *proof*, you can manually review&nbsp;the video frame-by-frame.

1. Go to the project, task, or issue that contains the document, then select `Documents`.
1. Find the *proof* you need, then click `Open *proof*`.

<ol start="3"> 
 <li value="3"> <p>At the bottom of the <em>proofing viewer</em>, click the <span class="bold">Forward</span>&nbsp;and <span class="bold">Back</span> arrows to review the video frame-by-frame.</p> <p> <img src="assets/frame-.png"> </p> </li> 
</ol>

## Change playback volume

You can control the volume in the video playback.

1. Go to the project, task, or issue that contains the document, then select `Documents`.
1. Find the *proof* you need, then click `Open *proof*`.

<ol start="3"> 
 <li value="3"> <p>In the lower-right corner of the <em>proofing viewer</em>, mouse over the <span class="bold">Volume</span> icon, then drag the slider to select a new volume.</p> <p> <img src="assets/phq-viewer-volume.png"> </p> <p>Or</p> <p> Click the <span class="bold">Volume</span> icon to mute and unmute the volume.<br></p> </li> 
</ol>
