# Viewing submitted proof approvals {#viewing-submitted-proof-approvals}

Proofing approvals display in the Home area only if your *`Adobe Workfront`* environment is integrated with a *`Workfront Proof`* Premium account. If you cannot use *`proofing`* as discussed here, contact your *`Workfront administrator`*.

## Viewing submitted proof approvals {#viewing-submitted-proof-approvals-1}




1.  Click the `Home` icon ![](assets/home-icon-30x29.png) in the upper-left corner of *`Adobe Workfront`*.
1. In the `Work List` area, Go to the `Approvals I've Submitted` grouping.

1. Select a `Proof` approval.  


   >[!NOTE]
   >
   >If you submit an approval to a guest *`proofing`* user, the approval doesn't display in Home.


  
   ![Proof.png](assets/proof-350x261.png)


1.  (Optional) Click `Go to Proof` in the upper-right corner of the right panel to launch the *`proofing`* viewer.
