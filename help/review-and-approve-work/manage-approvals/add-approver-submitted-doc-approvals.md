## Add additional approvers to submitted document approvals {#add-additional-approvers-to-submitted-document-approvals-1}




1.  Click the `Home` icon ![](assets/home-icon-30x29.png) in the upper-left corner of *`Adobe Workfront`*.
1. In the `Work List` area, Go to the `Approvals I've Submitted` grouping.

1. Select a `Document` approval.  

1. Click `Manage Approvals`&nbsp;in the upper-right corner of the right panel.
1.  In the `Have someone approve this document` box, type the name of the approver.


   If your *`Adobe Workfront administrator`* has enabled the capability to collaborate with people who don't use *`Workfront`*, as described in [Configure system security preferences](configure-security-preferences.md), you can type their email addresses to include them.

1. Click `Save`.
