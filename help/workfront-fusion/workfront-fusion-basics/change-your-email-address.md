## Change your email address

1. Click your username in the bottom of the left panel.
1. Click `Profile`.
1. Click the `Options` dropdown menu in the top right corner.
1. Click `Change email address`.
1. Type your new email address in the `Email` field.
1. Type your current password in the `Password` field.
1. Click `Save`.
