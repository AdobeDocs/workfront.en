You can view activity that has occurred on the proof. Activity includes recent changes made on the proof, including the date and description of the activity as well as the user who performed the action.

## View activity on a proof within Adobe Workfront

1. Select the document that contains the proof.
1. On the **Details** tab, under&nbsp;**Proofing**, click **Details**.

   ![View_activity_on_proof-click_Details.png](assets/view-activity-on-proof-click-details-350x217.png)

1. In the **Proofing Details** box that appears, expand the **Activity**&nbsp;section to display all activity that has occurred on the proof.
